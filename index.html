Basic Info for Hockey

Basic info

Hockey is a sport where you try to get more goals than the other team your facing within the time limt. There is 3 periods and the time in each period depends on your level of hockey. Hockey is a very popular sport and there are many teams you can play against. It is also a rough sport.

Equipement needed for hockey

You need many equiments to play hockey.
  • Hockey bag to carry your hockey equiment
  • Hockey sticks to manouvre the hockey puck and score goals
  • Hockey skates so you can move on the ice
  • Mouth guard(optional)shin pads, hockey pants, shoulder guard, elbow pads, hockey gloves, neckguard, jock, and a hockey helmet are all for protection
  • Jerseys and hockey socks are to differentiate the teams in hockey

    Hockey flex

    Hockey flex refers to how stiff your hockey stick is. If you have a low flex it will bend easier and you will release the puck of your stick faster, however you will have a slower shot. If you have a higher flex you would have a harder time bending your stick. This will make you be able to shoot stronger but, you will release the puck of your stick slower.
