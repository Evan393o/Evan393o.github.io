Hawaii

Travel to Hawaii

Hawaii is an island state part of the United States that has many good beaches. It is a big tourist attraction place that has many islands and it is always hot. There are also some volcanoes on some islands.

Reasons to travel to Hawaii

Beaches

Hawaii's beaches are all famous for their crystal clear waters, animals such as sea turtles and their sand. They have many types of sand colours such as black, green and red. This is because of the island's volcanic origins and the natural materials that make up the sand.

Famous beaches in Hawaii

Waikiki Beach

Honolua Bay

Poipu Beach

Food

Hawaii has many foods you rarely see in Canada. It has foods like Loco Moco, and Kalua pig. Sea foods such as poke, squid lau, and butterfish, as well as fruits like coconuts and bananas, are everywhere.
